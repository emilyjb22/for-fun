Welome to Paper People, a genetics simulation passion project by me, Emily Brereton.

This app attempts to replicate real human genetic inheritance. Users can create, randomize, and reproduce a series of computerized people. Genetic information is stored as a genotype, with a corresponding phenotype,
and genetic information is passed to offspring.

At this time, users can manually select or randomize traits, and users can select two parents (male and female) to make a baby. Individuals are stored to a population.

Eventually, users will be able to randomize large numbers of individuals, and statistically analyze population data over time.

Currently, individuals have hair color and eye color. I intend on adding skin color, hair texture, face shape, and other genetic attributes

Genetic information is loosely based on real-life inheritance patterns. Traints are polygenic, alleles are varying degrees of codominant
